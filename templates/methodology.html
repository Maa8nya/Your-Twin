How Your Digital Twin Thinks

The system analyzes behavioral patterns, decision-making styles, emotional responses, and risk tendencies to generate a cognitive Digital Twin simulation.

The system collects responses through psychologically designed scenario-based questions focused on habits, emotions, social behavior, uncertainty, and decision-making.

02

Cognitive Pattern Analysis

Each response is mapped to behavioral dimensions such as logical thinking, adaptability, planning style, emotional regulation, and social interaction.

03

Digital Twin Modeling

The behavioral signals are synthesized into a dynamic personality architecture that represents the user's Digital Twin profile.

04

AI Interpretation Layer

A Large Language Model analyzes the generated behavioral profile and converts it into intelligent human-readable insights and personality explanations.

05

Future Scenario Simulation

The Digital Twin predicts how the user may react under different real-world situations such as uncertainty, leadership pressure, emotional conflict, and high-risk opportunities.
