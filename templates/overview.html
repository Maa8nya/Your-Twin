Cognitive Digital Twin

What is Your Twin?

Your Twin is an AI-powered behavioral simulation system that creates a digital representation of your personality, decision-making style, emotional patterns, and social behavior using cognitive analysis.

Why It Matters

Human behavior is influenced by emotions, habits, risk tolerance, logic, and social interaction. Your Twin helps uncover these hidden behavioral patterns and transforms them into intelligent insights.

AI-Powered Intelligence

The system combines behavioral scoring, psychological modeling, and Large Language Models to generate human-like personality analysis and future behavioral simulations.

01

Behavioral Mapping

The system captures personality signals through scenario-based cognitive questions focused on decision-making, emotional response, and social interaction.

02

Twin Generation

AI models analyze behavioral dimensions such as logical thinking, adaptability, planning style, and risk tolerance to create a Digital Twin profile.

03

Future Simulation

Your Twin predicts how you may respond under real-world situations including leadership pressure, uncertainty, emotional conflict, and high-stakes decision-making.
